Ted Talk

Magazine spread

Ted Talk is a project where I used an existing magazine layout and repurposed it for presenting part of a transcript from a Ted Talk by Latif Nasser: You have no idea where camels really come from.

PRODUCT

PROCESS

To begin this project, I found a magazine spread I liked, which happened to be from Juxtapoz. I used this layout to inspire my final spread layout. The real challenge was trying to create a visual to represent the talk itself. I created over 20 sketches and eventually landed on the idea of having two camels in an hourglass. The top camel representing the ancestors of today's camels (in it's natural habitat) and the bottom representing today's camels. I photographed myself holding an hourglass and used Adobe Photoshop to edit the images together and laid the magazine out in Adobe InDesign.
