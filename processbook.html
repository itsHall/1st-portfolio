ART 323 Process Book

Process Book

Process Book is an overview of my process working through the various projects in Kofi Opoku's ART 323: Visual Communications at West Virginia University.

INTERACTIVE VIEW

PROCESS

This process book was designed to show my process while working in the Fall 2016 semester in Visual Communications at WVU. The key to this piece was organization through the semester. As well as compiling images from each project, I also colored TIFFs in Adobe Photoshop to show my sketches. I arranged the extent of my semester into an Adobe InDesign document and then printed, trimmed, and saddle-stitched it.
